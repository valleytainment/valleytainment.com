About Valleytainment

Our story, mission, and vision

Who We Are

Valleytainment Productions is a cutting-edge entertainment company founded in 2012 and established in 2018. We specialize in content creation, talent management, music/video production, videography, podcasting, graphic design, event planning, and more.

Our Mission

We're dedicated to pushing creative boundaries and delivering high-quality entertainment experiences that resonate with modern audiences. Our love & support for local talent and creativity sets us apart, and our monster character branding plus neon color scheme stands out in the industry.

Our Vision

To become the leading entertainment production company known for innovative content, exceptional talent management, and trend-setting merchandise that captures our unique brand identity, while showing love and support to all.
